General Rules and Guidelines

1. Draft Date

● Closest Sunday to the start of the season that is not Thanksgiving.

2. Drafting Position

● By reverse order of the finish of the preceding DaWiz (initially by the draw of cards).

● Based on those standings, owners get to choose their draft position for the current DaWiz (owner in last position gets first choice, owner in first position gets last choice).

● We will attempt to replace owner dropouts, and their draft order will be assumed by a new owner. See 10 for standings criteria.

3. Positional Requirements

● 2 centres, 2 left wingers, 2 right wingers, 2 defensemen for every team.

Positions are officially determined from the Bible (taken from NHL.com).

● No goalies (the Jim Carlaw Rule).
